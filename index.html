Common questions

Do I pay any fees or commissions?

Not on a cash sale. No agent commissions, no closing-cost surprises, no service fees. The offer you accept is what you walk away with, minus whatever you still owe on the property.

How fast can you actually close?

As fast as about two weeks for a cash purchase — and if you need more time, we close on your schedule instead. You pick the date.

My house needs a lot of work. Is that a problem?

No. We buy houses completely as-is — repairs needed, full of stuff, tenants in place, whatever the situation. You don't fix or clean anything.

My property has liens or a court sale date coming up. Can you still buy it?

Usually, yes — and timing matters a lot in that situation. A sale that closes before an auction date lets you collect the equity you've built instead of losing it. Reach out with your date and we'll tell you honestly what's possible in the time available.

Am I obligated to accept your offer?

Never. The offer is free, and "no thanks" is a perfectly fine answer. If listing the home is the better move for you, we'll say so.
